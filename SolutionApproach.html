EQUALITY PROJECT - EQUITABLE PAY OPPORTUNITIES

  • Access to politics: Ensure equal participation of men and women in political decision-making, promoting equal representation at all levels of government.
  • Working parenthood: Promote policies that enable fathers to share family and work responsibilities equally, recognising the value of shared care and parenting.
  • Women's suffrage: Defend and strengthen women's right to vote as a fundamental pillar of democracy and equal rights.
  • Housework: Promote the equal sharing of domestic and childcare responsibilities, recognising unpaid work as an invaluable contribution to family and social well-being.
  • Overcoming the wage gap: Implement measures to eliminate the wage gap between men and women, ensuring equal pay for work of equal value.
  • Equity in access to education: Ensure equal access to education for all people, regardless of gender, ethnicity or socio-economic status.
  • Right to food: Ensure that all people, especially women and girls, have access to adequate and nutritious food as a fundamental right for their development and well-being.
  • Women's Right to Education: Promote women's access to and retention in all levels of education, from basic education to higher education and vocational training, removing any barriers that may impede their full and equal participation.
